    Club Mahindra Organizes Blood Donation Camp

    On the 70th Foundation Day, Club Mahindra Udaipur organized blood donation camp on 1st October with the help of Lokmitra Bood Bank and Hindu Help Line. The workers at Club Mahindra participated in the camp with enthusiasm and were motivated by Chief Manager Sanjog Sahani. Total 24 units of blood were donated in the camp. Hindu Help Line?s Gaurav, Pradeep Mathur and Honey Singh were present along with Club Mahindra?s Wilson.


    Lions Club Eklingji: 63 members donate blood

    Lions Club Eklingji with North Western Railway Institute organized blood donation and diabetes check up camp on the eve of Gandhi Jayanti on 2nd October at Thokar Circle. President Naresh Maheshwari informed that 63 units of blood were collected in the camp and around 130 people attended the diabetes check up camp. Rajendra Jindal, Kamlesh Kumawat, Bhagwatilal, Surendra Mogra, Tarun Bhandari, Dinesh Sukhwal and many others were part of the blood donation camp. All the donors were honored by the club along with all the medical staff and railway staff members by Deputy Governor Arvind Chatur and Deepak Hingad.


    Blood Donation in remembrance of Imam Hussain

    Annual blood donation camp was organized today by Bohra Youth Medical Relief Society to mark the martyrdom of Imam Hussain (AS) the grandson of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). In all 172 units of blood were donated by the volunteers and community members at Bohra Youth Dispensary, Bohrawadi. The camp was started early morning and continued till late in the evening. MB Hospital and Saral Blood Bank partnered the event, which was supported by HDFC Bank.

Ramada Udaipur?s initiative is a part of its commitment to participate and support the social welfare programs, in particular the humanitarian programs. Many of the resort?s employees and their family took part in this initiative. In all 41 units of blood was donated today from Ramada Udaipur?s staff and Management.
